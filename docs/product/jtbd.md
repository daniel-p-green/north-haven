## Core Job Statement

When I am confused or worried about a text, account, or device issue, I want clear, step-by-step help in the messages or calls I already use, so I can fix the issue safely without feeling overwhelmed.

## Primary Jobs

1. Help me resolve common tech problems quickly.
2. Help me spot scams and avoid risky actions.
3. Let me move to a voice call when text is not enough.
4. Keep my family informed only when risk is high and I approve.

## Job Steps

| Step | Current friction | Desired progress |
|---|---|---|
| Discover | Unsure if issue is urgent or scam | Fast triage with clear risk label |
| Decide | Too many options, unclear next action | One safe step at a time |
| Execute | Confusion and repeated failures | Guided loop with voice escalation |
| Verify | Not sure if issue is fully resolved | Concise completion summary |
| Maintain | Re-explaining history every time | Consentful memory and thread continuity |

## Outcome Expectations

- Speed: immediate first response and reliable callback path.
- Confidence: simple explanations and explicit safety checks.
- Control: clear consent controls for memory and Care Circle.

## Forces of Adoption

### Push (pain)

- Scam fear and account lockouts.
- Low confidence in self-service support flows.

### Pull (benefits)

- Trusted contact model in familiar channels.
- Calm, plain-language guidance and safe escalation.

### Anxiety (risk)

- Worry about privacy, spammy alerts, and false confidence.

### Habits (status quo)

- Calling family, searching random forums, or ignoring suspicious messages.
